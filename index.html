How it works

Three steps. Five minutes. Done.

No app to download. No login. No form. Just send a text and answer a few questions.

01

Text the Debrief+ number

Send a message to your organization's Debrief+ number. The AI introduces itself, confirms confidentiality, and invites you to describe what happened in your own words.

02

Have a conversation

The AI asks targeted follow-up questions — one at a time, short messages. It knows your industry and asks the right things without overwhelming you.

03

Reply REPORT

When you're done, reply REPORT. A structured report is generated and sent directly to your safety committee's database. You're done.

Why it matters

Incidents are underreported.
That's a systemic problem.

When people don't report, safety committees can't see patterns. Recurring issues go undetected. The same problems keep happening.

01

Asset-specific tracking

Every report captures the specific asset, vehicle, or location involved. Safety committees can see which equipment or sites are repeat offenders.

02

Pattern analysis

When conditions are documented consistently, patterns emerge across teams, shifts, and facilities that would otherwise stay invisible.

03

Better maintenance records

The AI coaches teams to write detailed, specific reports — forcing more thorough follow-up rather than a quick check and sign-off.

04

Reported while details are fresh

Traditional forms get filled out hours later, if at all. A text conversation in the field captures details that would otherwise be lost.

05

Structured data from day one

Every report lands in a structured database, ready for trend analysis and dashboards — not a pile of unreadable PDFs.

06

Builds trust in the process

When people know reporting is confidential and non-punitive, they actually do it. Debrief+ is designed to earn that trust from the first message.
